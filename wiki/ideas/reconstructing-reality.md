# Reconstructing Reality

## The Insight
The pivot from "generating synthetic imagery" (creating things that don't exist) to "reconstructing reality" (using generative latent space mastery to create high-fidelity 3D maps of existing physical biological structures from sparse data).

## Evidence
- [[entities/midjourney-medical]] — The full-body ultrasonic scanner that produces 3D body maps in <60 seconds, utilizing generative reconstruction to achieve MRI-like detail with ultrasound speed.

## Implications
This transforms generative AI from a creative tool into a high-precision medical diagnostic tool. It suggests that the same "denoising" and "filling" capabilities used in art can be used to reconstruct physical reality from low-resolution sensors.

## Connections
- [[sources/midjourney]] — The strategic pivot of the company from art to medical hardware.
- [[ideas/physical-ai-pivot]] — A prime example of an AI company moving from pure software to integrated hardware-software reality reconstruction.
