# Open Platform AI

## The Insight
Three major moves on May 25, 2026 signal a convergence toward open AI ecosystems: OpenAI's PowerPoint add-in embeds ChatGPT into Microsoft Office, Apple's iOS 27 Extensions framework will let users choose their preferred AI model over Siri, and Google's CEO publicly acknowledged the company's agentic gap. The trend is clear — the walled garden approach is giving way to platform openness. Even Google, which built the best models but missed the agentic workflow layer, is pivoting to acknowledge its shortcomings.

## Implications
The open platform trend reflects several dynamics:
1. **Market pressure** — Claude's enterprise lead (34.4% vs 32.3%) shows safety/compliance narrative winning
2. **Regulatory anticipation** — EU AI Act and US state laws pushing transparency
3. **Developer expectations** — MCP and open protocols standardizing cross-platform tool use
4. **Monopoly erosion** — Single-provider AI becoming untenable as use cases mature

Companies that maintain closed ecosystems risk being perceived as anti-choice — especially Apple, which built its brand on integration. The shift to open platforms means AI vendors must compete on quality, not lock-in.
