# De Novo Generative Biology

## The Insight
AI in biology has moved beyond "AI-assisted optimization" (tweaking existing proteins) to "de novo" design (creating entirely new biological structures from scratch). This is drastically shrinking the cycle from hypothesis to human trial, moving the timeline from years to months.

## Evidence
- University of Cambridge researchers began human trials of a universal coronavirus vaccine designed entirely by AI in June 2026.

## Implications
This signals the start of a "generative era" for medicine where vaccines and drugs are designed as software. The speed of iteration will likely lead to a surge in "personalized" or "rapid-response" medicine, where a vaccine for a new variant can be designed and trialed in weeks.
