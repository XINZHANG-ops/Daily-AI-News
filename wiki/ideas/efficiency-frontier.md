# Efficiency Frontier

## The Insight
The AI industry is discovering that raw capability scaling is not the only path — efficiency breakthroughs can achieve domain dominance with dramatically less compute. Qwen 3.6-35B-A3B's 3 billion active parameters scoring 73.4% on SWE-Bench demonstrates that right-sized models can compete with models 20x their size. This has profound implications for who can participate in the AI race and where value accumulates.

## Implications
The efficiency frontier democratizes access to high-quality AI — models that once required data center resources can now run on consumer devices. This shifts the competitive battlefield from谁能 build the biggest模型 to 谁 can achieve the best capability-to-parameter ratio.

For enterprises, efficient models mean lower inference costs and faster time-to-deployment. For consumers, on-device AI becomes viable without cloud dependencies. For geopolitics, efficiency advantages could rebalance the US-China competition if Chinese labs can achieve parity at lower compute costs.
