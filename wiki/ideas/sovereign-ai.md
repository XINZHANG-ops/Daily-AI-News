# Sovereign AI

## The Insight
Sovereign AI is the strategic imperative for nations and organizations to own, run, and maintain their own high-capability AI models independently of foreign cloud infrastructure. It is the transition from AI as a "service" (SaaS) to AI as a "national utility" or "strategic asset."

## Evidence
- [[entities/dataroom]] — A framework specifically designed to use local models (Qwen3.6) for grounded research, ensuring data and capability sovereignty.
- [[entities/neuralbridge-sdk]] — Focuses on "self-healing" and resilience against external API failures, acknowledging that the cloud frontier is volatile.
- [[ideas/ai-iron-curtain]] — The a-priori necessity for sovereign AI is driven by the emergence of the AI Iron Curtain and the "digital kill switch."

## Implications
1. **Shift in Metrics**: The focus moves from "maximum raw capability" (SOTA) to "maximum capability per watt/parameter" to make local execution feasible.
2. **Infrastructure Investment**: Massive state-level investment in local compute clusters and energy-efficient hardware.
3. **Open-Weights Primacy**: Open-weight models (like the Gemma 4 family or Qwen) become the foundational building blocks of sovereign stacks.
