# The Digital Kill Switch

## The Insight
The "Digital Kill Switch" refers to the capacity of a state actor (specifically the US government) to exercise absolute control over the availability of frontier AI weights, disabling them globally and instantaneously for specific populations or entirely. This is a shift from *voluntary* safety pauses by labs to *mandated* pruning of cognitive capabilities by the state.

## Evidence
- [[sources/anthropic]] — US Department of Commerce ordered the immediate global suspension of **Fable 5** and **Mythos 5** for all foreign nationals in June 2026.
- [[ideas/ai-iron-curtain]] — The resulting gap in capability between US-aligned and non-US-aligned regions creates a new form of geopolitical inequality.

## Implications
1. **Weight Volatility**: The most capable models are no longer "utilities" but "political assets." Relying on them for critical infrastructure is now a strategic risk.
2. **Incentive for Localism**: This accelerates the move toward high-performance open-weight models and local-first agent architectures.
3. **State Control**: It establishes a precedent where the government, not the lab, decides when a model is "too capable" to exist in certain jurisdictions.
