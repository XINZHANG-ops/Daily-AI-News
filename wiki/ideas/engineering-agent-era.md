# Engineering Agent Era

## The Insight
The evolution of AI coding from "Copilots" (autocomplete) $\rightarrow$ "Coding Agents" (writing scripts) $\rightarrow$ "Engineering Agents" (designing and managing complex, multi-component physical and digital systems).

## Evidence
- [[sources/spacex]] — The $60B acquisition of Cursor to move from a code editor to an aerospace engineering pipeline.
- [[entities/prometheus-ai]] — A dedicated project to build a "General Engineer" for the physical world.

## Implications
This represents the final step in the automation of intellectual labor in the STEM fields. When AI can manage the design, simulation, and optimization of physical infrastructure, the traditional engineering career path is fundamentally disrupted, shifting focus toward high-level systems architecture and strategic oversight.
