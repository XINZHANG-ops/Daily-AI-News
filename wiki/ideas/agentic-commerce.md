# Agentic Commerce

## The Insight
The transition of e-commerce from "search and filter" (grids of products) to "conversational discovery" (agents that understand needs and curate options). The user stops searching for an image or keyword and starts discussing a need with an agent.

## Evidence
- [[sources/pinterest]] — The launch of "Ask Pinterest," a conversational shopping app that replaces the visual grid with a chatbot-like interface for personalized recommendations.

## Implications
The "death of the grid" UI. Commerce becomes a dialogue. This shifts the value from the search algorithm to the agent's ability to understand nuance and personal preference, effectively moving the curation layer from the user's manual effort to the AI's autonomous capability.
