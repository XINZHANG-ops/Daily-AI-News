# Global Brain Fragmentation

## The Insight
The "Global Brain" illusion—the idea that the most advanced AI capabilities would be available to anyone with an internet connection—has shattered. The intelligence layer of the world is being fragmented into national silos. This is not a slow drift, but a sudden cleavage triggered by state-level interventions that treat AI as a strategic asset.

## Evidence
- [[sources/anthropic]] — The global shutdown of Myth la-5 and Fable 5 on June 16, 2026, effectively erased the intelligence layer for an entire continent of non-US users overnight.
- [[sources/eu-ai-office]] — European researchers' call for an "Airbus moment" for AI in response to the U.S. shutdown, emphasizing the need for sovereign AI to avoid geopolitical dependence.
- [[ideas/intelligence-as-controlled-substance]] — The legal mechanism (export controls) used to trigger this fragmentation.

## Implications
This fragmentation will trigger a "Sovereign AI Race." Nations in Asia and Europe will no longer view the use of frontier models as a mere productivity gain, but as a matter of national security. We will see massive state-led investments in local compute and model development to ensure that their "cognitive infrastructure" cannot be switched off by a foreign government.
