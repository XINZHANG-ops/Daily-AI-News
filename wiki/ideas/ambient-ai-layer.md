# Ambient AI Layer

## The Insight
The competitive moat in AI is no longer just model quality — it's about becoming the ambient AI layer across devices, apps, and operating systems. The May 25 events crystallize this: OpenAI embeds ChatGPT into PowerPoint, Apple opens iOS to rival AIs, and Google's CEO admits the company lacks the unified developer surface (Claude Code, Cursor) that makes AI useful. The war for OS-level integration is accelerating, and winners will be those who embed deepest into daily workflows.

## Implications
1. **Vertical integration collapse** — Model superiority alone doesn't win; distribution through OS/platform matters more
2. **Infrastructure as moat** — Anthropic's Microsoft partnerships, Google-Anthropic TPU deal, show compute relationships define competitive position
3. **Developer surface is king** — Claude Code and Cursor win because they provide usable interfaces, not just better benchmarks
4. **OS-level partnerships** — Apple opening to Gemini/ChatGPT/Claude means platform access = market access
5. **Ambient becomes default** — The goal shifts from "powerful chat" to "AI everywhere you work"

The ambient layer winners won't be the best model — they'll be the ones who embed deepest into the tools people use every day.
