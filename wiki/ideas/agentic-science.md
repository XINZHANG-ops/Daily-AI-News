# Agentic Science

## The Insight
The transition from "AI for research" (where AI analyzes data or suggests papers) to "Agentic Science," where the AI owns the entire loop: hypothesis $\rightarrow$ experiment design $\rightarrow$ physical execution $\rightarrow$ analysis $\rightarrow$ refined hypothesis.

## Evidence
- [[entities/maria-agent]] — Successfully improved the Chan-Lam coupling reaction by running 10,000+ automated physical experiments in a high-throughput lab.
- [[entities/gpt-5.4]] — Provided the reasoning and search capabilities to guide the Maria agent through the chemical space.

## Implications
Drug discovery and material science cycles are compressed from months to days. The role of the human scientist shifts from "doer" to "curator" and "strategic director," while the AI acts as the lead chemist.

## Connections
- [[ideas/conceptual-leaps]] — Agentic science relies on the AI's ability to make conceptual leaps about which additives (like TEMPO) might work, rather than just brute-forcing.
- [[topics/agentic_ai]] — The highest-value application of agentic AI, moving it from digital productivity to physical-world discovery.
