# Midjourney

## Overview
Midjourney is primarily known as a leader in generative AI art. In June 2026, the company underwent a shocking strategic pivot into medical hardware with the launch of "Midjourney Medical," applying its expertise in latent space and generative reconstruction to the physical biological world.
