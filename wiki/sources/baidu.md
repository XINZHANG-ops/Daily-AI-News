# Baidu

## Overview
Baidu launched Ernie 5.1 on May 9, 2026, claiming pre-training costs at just 6% of industry peers at similar scale. The model compresses parameters to one-third of Ernie 5.0 while surpassing DeepSeek-V4-Pro on agent capabilities and ranking 4th globally on LMArena search. This positions Baidu as a leading efficiency innovator in the Chinese AI market, challenging both domestic rivals and Western frontier labs on cost-performance metrics.
