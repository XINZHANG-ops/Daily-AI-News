# Allen Institute for AI (Ai2)

## Overview
Ai2 is a Seattle-based AI research organization founded by the late Paul Allen. It is best known for OLMo, one of the most transparent open-source language models in the industry — releasing not just weights but complete training data, code, and methodologies. On May 16, 2026, Microsoft hired at least 10 core OLMo researchers including former CEO Ali Farhadi, COO Sophie Lebrecht, and research leaders Hanna Hajishirzi and Ranjay Krishna, fundamentally altering the organization's trajectory.
