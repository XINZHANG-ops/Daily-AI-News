# Vast Data

## Overview
Vast Data is an AI infrastructure startup backed by NVIDIA that provides data storage and management solutions optimized for AI workloads. The company raised $1 billion at a $30 billion valuation in April 2026, reflecting continued investor appetite for AI infrastructure beyond compute and models. NVIDIA's backing (along with investments in OpenAI, Anthropic, and xAI) signals a strategy to control multiple layers of the AI infrastructure stack.
