# Thinking Machines Lab

## Overview
Thinking Machines Lab is Mira Murati's AI startup, formed after her departure from OpenAI. The lab shipped its first public model, TML-Interaction-Small, on May 11, 2026 — a 276B-parameter MoE model with 12B active parameters designed for real-time multimodal interaction at 200ms latency. The lab is betting that cost efficiency at scale beats raw capability for voice-first applications, directly challenging OpenAI's GPT-Realtime-2 with a cheaper-to-run architecture.
