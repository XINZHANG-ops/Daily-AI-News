# Isomorphic Labs

## Overview
Isomorphic Labs is Google's AI-powered drug design subsidiary, spun out from DeepMind to apply AlphaFold-derived models to pharmaceutical discovery. The company is revolutionizing drug discovery by using AI to predict protein structures and design new therapeutic molecules. In May 2026, it raised the largest AI biotech funding round of the year.

## Significance
The $2.1B funding round is extraordinary even by 2026 standards. The investor lineup — Thrive Capital (top AI investor), Alphabet itself, Temasek (Singapore sovereign wealth fund), and the UK Sovereign AI Fund — suggests this is as much about strategic positioning as capital. Isomorphic's approach represents one of the most tangible "AI solves real problems" narratives in the industry. Compared to Recursive Superintelligence's $650M raise the same week for "autonomous training systems," drug discovery has clear metrics and credible near-term applications.
