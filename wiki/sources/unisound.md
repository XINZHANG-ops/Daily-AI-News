# Unisound

## Overview
Unisound (云知声) is a Hong Kong-listed Chinese AI company that released U2 on June 8, 2026 — a "native execution" agentic model claiming GPQA Diamond 87.9, positioning above GLM-5.1, DeepSeek-V4-Flash, and MiniMax-M2.7. The company is the first Hong Kong-listed frontier model vendor to ship a serious agentic model, and U2's commercial distribution via the Unisound Token Hub (MaaS, not open weights) makes it the test case for whether a Chinese frontier model company can out-earn DeepSeek's open-weight strategy by selling tokens instead of giving them away.
