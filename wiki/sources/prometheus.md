# Prometheus

## Overview
Prometheus is a Jeff Bezos-backed AI venture focused on creating an "Artificial General Engineer" for the physical world. The company aims to automate the design and manufacturing of complex physical systems, such as jet engines, by bridging the "sim-to-real" gap at an industrial scale.
