# Prometheus

## Overview
Prometheus is an ambitious AI venture and product funded by Jeff Bezos, aiming to build an "artificial general engineer" capable of operating and executing tasks in the physical world. It focuses on the intersection of high-level reasoning and physical manipulation, bridging the "sim-to-real" gap at an industrial scale.

## Significance
Prometheus signals a major pivot in the AGI race: a shift from the "digital brain" (LLMs) to the "physical body" (embodied agency). The $12B investment suggests that the next frontier of AI is not just more parameters or better reasoning, but the ability to interact with and manipulate the physical environment at a general level, automating the design and manufacturing of complex physical systems (e.g., jet engines).
