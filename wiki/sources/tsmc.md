# TSMC

## Overview
Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) is the world's critical AI chip foundry, manufacturing AI accelerators for NVIDIA, AMD, Google (TPU), and Amazon (Trainium). On April 29, TSMC announced a temporary pause on new AI accelerator orders for Q3 2026 delivery after a yield crisis in its 3nm process node spread from consumer chips to NVIDIA H200 and AMD MI350 wafers. Yield rates reportedly dropped from 75% to 52% — meaning nearly half of every H200 wafer is scrap.
