# Tomoro

## What It Is
Tomoro is an AI deployment company that OpenAI acquired in May 2026 as part of its launch of the Deployment Company. The acquisition brought 150 experienced forward deployed engineers who specialize in embedding AI into enterprise workflows. The deal was valued as part of OpenAI's over $4 billion initial investment in its new deployment business.
