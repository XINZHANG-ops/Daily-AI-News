# Samsung

## Overview
Samsung is a major South Korean technology conglomerate and one of the world's largest memory chip manufacturers. In May 2026, Samsung became a strategic investor in Anthropic's $65 billion funding round, joining SK Hynix and Micron as chip suppliers with equity stakes in AI companies they supply.
