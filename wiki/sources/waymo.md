# Waymo

## Overview
Waymo is Google's autonomous vehicle subsidiary, operating robotaxi services in multiple US cities. The company achieved a major milestone with the Ojai sixth-generation vehicle, proving unit economics for autonomous ride-hailing.

## Timeline

| Date | Event | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 2026-05-28 | Ojai robotaxi launch | Sixth-generation vehicle with 42% fewer sensors, $75K cost reduction per vehicle |
| 2026-05-31 | Unit economics proof | $125K vehicle cost vs $200K previous; expansion to Denver, Vegas, San Diego announced |

## Key Developments

- **Ojai Generation**: 13 cameras, 4 lidar, 6 radar — 42% fewer sensors than previous generation
- **Cost Reduction**: Vehicle cost dropped from $200K to $125K per unit
- **Platform**: Built on Geely Zeekr platform (base vehicle $38K vs $75K Jaguar)
- **Expansion**: Denver, Las Vegas, San Diego planned
- **Hardware Cost**: ~$20,000 — >50% reduction enabling fleet-scale profitability
