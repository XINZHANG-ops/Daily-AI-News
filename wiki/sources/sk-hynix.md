# SK Hynix

## Overview
SK Hynix is a major South Korean memory chip manufacturer and leading supplier of High Bandwidth Memory (HBM) critical for AI training. In May 2026, SK Hynix became a strategic investor in Anthropic's $65 billion funding round, joining Samsung and Micron as chip suppliers with equity stakes in AI companies they supply.
