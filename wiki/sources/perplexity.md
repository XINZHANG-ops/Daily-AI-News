# Perplexity

## Overview
Perplexity has grown from an AI search engine to a full-stack AI platform. April 2026 saw the company achieve 5x revenue growth to $500M while only increasing headcount by 34%, signaling that AI-first products are achieving revenue scale with lean teams. The launch of "Perplexity Computer" — an always-on AI agent running locally on Mac mini at $200/month — marks the company's pivot from search into always-on agentic AI.
