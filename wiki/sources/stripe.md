# Stripe

## Overview
Stripe has positioned itself as the essential financial infrastructure for the emerging agent economy. At Stripe Sessions 2026, the company announced 288 new products centered on "agentic commerce" — the most comprehensive financial infrastructure launch for autonomous AI agents to date. Key innovations include Link Wallets for Agents (one-time-use virtual cards that prevent AI agents from accessing raw payment credentials), Streaming Payments on the Tempo blockchain for real-time token billing, and expanded Radar fraud protection specifically targeting AI-specific abuse. Partnerships with Google AI Mode and Gemini join existing OpenAI, Microsoft, and Meta integrations, meaning Stripe now powers checkout inside every major AI assistant.
