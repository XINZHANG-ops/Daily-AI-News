# Micron

## Overview
Micron Technology is a major American memory chip manufacturer and leading supplier of DRAM and NAND memory for AI infrastructure. In May 2026, Micron became a strategic investor in Anthropic's $65 billion funding round, joining Samsung and SK Hynix as chip suppliers with equity stakes in AI companies they supply.
