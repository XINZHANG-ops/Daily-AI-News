# Netomi

## Overview
Netomi is an agentic AI customer experience platform that deploys autonomous agents for enterprise customer service at scale. The company processes up to 40,000 requests per second for clients including Delta, DraftKings, NBA, and MetLife. In May 2026, Netomi raised $110M Series C led by Accenture Ventures with Adobe Ventures and Silver Lake participating, bringing total funding to over $160M.
