# Vercel

## Overview
Vercel is the company behind the Next.js web framework and Vercel AI SDK, known for making AI application deployment seamless. The company has expanded into agentic AI tooling with ZeroLang, a programming language designed specifically for AI agents.

- **Anthropic**: Vercel AI SDK supports Claude models
