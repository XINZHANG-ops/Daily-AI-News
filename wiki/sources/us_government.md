# U.S. Government

## Role in AI
The U.S. Government has transitioned from a purely regulatory role to a direct strategic actor in the AI ecosystem, treating frontier AI capabilities as critical national security assets.

## Key Actions
- **Export Controls**: Use of emergency directives to deactivate AI models globally to prevent proliferation to adversaries (e.g., June 15, 2026).
- **Strategic Partnerships**: Negotiating directly with AI labs (e.g., Anthropic) to manage model access and restoration.
- **Equity Stakes**: Exploring government equity stakes in foundational AI firms to ensure national utility and oversight.
