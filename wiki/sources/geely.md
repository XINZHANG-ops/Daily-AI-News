# Geely

## Overview
Geely is a Chinese automotive group that owns Volvo, Polestar, Zeekr, and other brands. The company partners with Waymo to build the Zeekr platform for the Ojai robotaxi.

## Key Developments

- **Zeekr Platform**: Base vehicle costs $38K vs $75K Jaguar I-PACE used in previous generation
- **Waymo Partnership**: Manufacturing partner for Waymo's sixth-generation robotaxi
- **Cost Impact**: Enables $75K cost reduction per vehicle, proving unit economics
