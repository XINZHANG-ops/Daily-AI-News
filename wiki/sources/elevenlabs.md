# ElevenLabs

## Overview
ElevenLabs is the leading voice AI company, announcing $500M ARR (up from $350M at start of 2026) and a Series D at an $11 billion valuation. New investors include BlackRock, Nvidia NVentures, Salesforce Ventures, and celebrities Jamie Foxx and Squid Game creator Hwang Dong-hyuk. The company opened a Sydney HQ for ANZ expansion with 750K+ users in the region.
