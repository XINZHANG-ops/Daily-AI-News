# Qualcomm

## Overview
Qualcomm is a major US semiconductor and telecommunications equipment company, best known for its Snapdragon mobile processors and 5G modem technology. In the AI era, Qualcomm has positioned its silicon as the platform for on-device AI inference, particularly for mobile, edge, and now agentic-device form factors.
