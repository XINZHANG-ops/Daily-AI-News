# Allen Control Systems

## Overview
Allen Control Systems is a US defense technology company that raised a $200M Series B at a $2.2B valuation on June 8, 2026, led by Smash Capital with Craft Ventures, Rally Ventures, and Inspired Capital participating. The company produces Bullfrog, an AI-guided autonomous counter-drone system that fuses computer vision, robotics, and AI decision-making to neutralize small unmanned aerial systems. Bullfrog is deployed with the US Army and US Navy and achieved a 100% success rate in the T-REX 26-1 defense exercise. The $2.2B valuation represents the largest pure-physical-AI defense funding event of June 2026 outside the Pentagon's classified contracts.
