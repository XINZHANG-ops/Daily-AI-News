# xAI

## Overview
xAI has been consolidated into SpaceX following the $250 billion all-stock acquisition, creating a $1.25 trillion "galactic AI entity." Grok 4 remains under development with Q1 2026 release target missed, and Grok 5 has not launched despite passing the Q1 2026 window. The combined entity is investing $60 billion in Cursor, directly competing with Anthropic's Claude Code and OpenAI's Codex.
