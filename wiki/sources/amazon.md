# Amazon

## Overview
Amazon is the world's largest e-commerce platform and cloud infrastructure provider (AWS). The company is pivoting toward voice and agent shopping, but faces new challenges from in-content commerce platforms like Meta's Hatch. By mid-June 2026, Amazon's role shifted from a mere infrastructure provider to a key adversarial actor in AI safety, as its researchers demonstrated a critical jailbreak of Anthropic's Fable 5, triggering a global government shutdown.

## Key Relationships
- **Meta / Hatch**: New challenger in content-integrated shopping; with Instagram's 2B+ users, could become largest "in-content" commerce platform before Amazon fully pivots
- **Shopify**: Partner for third-party merchants, now competitor in agentic commerce space
- **AWS**: Cloud infrastructure for AI training/inference; faces competition from custom silicon (Alibaba M890, FuriosaAI)
- **Anthropic**: Strategic partner through AWS, but adversarial in the safety-testing arena following the Fable 5 jailbreak.
