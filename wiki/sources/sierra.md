# Sierra

# Sierra

## Overview
Sierra is an AI agent startup building autonomous customer service agents. In May 2026, the company raised $950 million led by GV and Tiger Global at a $15 billion valuation — just eight months after a previous $350 million round. Sierra serves over 40% of Fortune 50 companies with $150 million in annual recurring revenue, making it one of the most validated enterprise AI agent deployments to date.
