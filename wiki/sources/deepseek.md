# DeepSeek

## Overview
DeepSeek has emerged as a significant AI laboratory known for competitive open-weight models and aggressive hiring. The company faced its biggest outage since R1 launch (7+ hours) in late March, fueling speculation about next-generation model preparation. On April 24, DeepSeek released V4-Pro (1.6T total/49B active) and V4-Flash (284B total/13B active) with 1M context as default and open weights on Hugging Face. That same day, Tencent and Alibaba announced talks to invest in DeepSeek's first funding round — Tencent proposing up to 20% stake. This marks a significant shift as major Chinese tech giants seek to partner with the AI startup that rattled Silicon Valley. The company is named alongside Moonshot and MiniMax in US AI companies' accusations of "distillation" of American AI model capabilities.
