# Coinbase

## Overview
Coinbase is the largest US cryptocurrency exchange. On May 5, 2026, CEO Brian Armstrong announced a 14% workforce reduction — approximately 660 to 700 employees — citing both a crypto market downturn and AI rapidly changing how the company operates. Armstrong stated engineers now use AI to "ship in days what used to take a team weeks." The restructuring includes flattening management layers, experimenting with "one-person teams," and shifting some roles toward managing AI agents. Estimated restructuring cost is $50-60 million.
