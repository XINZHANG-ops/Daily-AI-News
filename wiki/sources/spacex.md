# SpaceX

## Overview
SpaceX acquired xAI for $250 billion in an all-stock transaction in April 2026, creating a $1.25 trillion "galactic AI entity." The combined entity aims to leverage Starlink's satellite network for low-latency global AI distribution and Tesla's robotics expertise for physical AI deployment. SpaceX (which owns AI assistant Grok) is now directly competing with Anthropic's Claude Code, OpenAI's Codex, and vibe-coding startups such as Cursor, Lovable, Emergent, and Bolt through a $60 billion investment in Cursor.
