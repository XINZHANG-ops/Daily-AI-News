# SpaceX

## Overview
SpaceX acquired xAI for $250 billion in an all-stock transaction in April 2026, creating a $1.25 trillion "galactic AI entity." On May 6, 2026, SpaceX signed a compute partnership with Anthropic providing 300+ megawatts of capacity at SpaceX's Colossus 1 data center in Memphis — a surprising alliance given Musk's prior criticism of Anthropic. Simultaneously, xAI was dissolved and rebranded as SpaceXAI, consolidating Musk's AI bets under one roof. The combined entity aims to leverage Starlink's satellite network for low-latency global AI distribution and Tesla's robotics expertise for physical AI deployment. SpaceX (which owns AI assistant Grok) is now directly competing with Anthropic's Claude Code, OpenAI's Codex, and vibe-coding startups such as Cursor, Lovable, Emergent, and Bolt through a $60 billion investment in Cursor.
