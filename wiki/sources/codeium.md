# Codeium

## Overview
Codeium is the company behind Windsurf, an AI-powered coding IDE that competes with Cursor, Claude Code, and OpenAI Codex. Codeium's key differentiator is its "always-on" cloud execution model, allowing agents to continue running even after local devices shut down. The company raised significant funding to compete in the AI coding tool market.
