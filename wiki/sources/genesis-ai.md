# Genesis AI

## Overview
Genesis AI is a French AI startup specializing in pragmatic, industrial-scale embodied agency. Unlike many of its competitors who pursue the "humanoid" ideal, Genesis AI focuses on optimizing robots for industrial ROI, blending high-dexterity manipulation with efficient mobility.

## Key Relationships
Genesis AI represents the European approach to embodied AI: focusing on industrial application and pragmatic design over the "general-purpose humanoid" race dominated by US and Chinese labs.
