# Singapore

## Overview
Singapore is positioning itself as the strategic gateway between US AI giants and Southeast Asia's 700M+ market. On May 21, 2026, Singapore announced major partnerships with OpenAI, Google, and Nvidia — marking a significant escalation in the city-state's AI hub ambitions.

## Key Relationships
Singapore wins not just investment but talent concentration — the OpenAI Applied AI Lab is the company's first research facility outside the US. This mirrors Hong Kong's financial-services play but for AI infrastructure.
