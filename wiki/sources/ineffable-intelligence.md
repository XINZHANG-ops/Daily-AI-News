# Ineffable Intelligence

## Overview
Ineffable Intelligence is an AI startup founded by ex-DeepMind researcher David Silver (co-author of the AlphaGo paper) that raised the largest seed round in AI history at $1.1 billion in April 2026. The company has not disclosed what it is building, but the investor syndicate includes Sequoia, Lightspeed, Nvidia, Google, and DST Global. Within 72 hours of funding, the company hired 50 senior researchers from DeepMind, OpenAI, and Anthropic — AlphaGo, AlphaFold, and GPT-4 paper authors among them.
