# Cognition

## Overview
Cognition is an AI company creating Devin — an autonomous AI software engineer agent. In May 2026, Cognition raised $1B at $26B valuation, more than doubling from September 2025. The company's "program synthesis" approach differentiates it from generalist LLMs, competing directly with Claude Code, Codex, and the emerging Mistral Vibe.
