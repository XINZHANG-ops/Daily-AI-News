# Microsoft

## Overview
Microsoft has emerged as a major AI infrastructure and platform player, integrating Claude Sonnet into Microsoft 365 Copilot while building its own independent model capabilities (MAI-Transcribe, MAI-Voice, MAI-Image). The company's Agent Framework v1.0 and partnership with NVIDIA position it as an enterprise AI platform. $10 billion committed to Japan AI infrastructure represents the largest Western bet on Asian AI.
