# Shopify

## Overview
Shopify is the leading e-commerce platform powering millions of merchants worldwide. The company is positioning itself for "agentic commerce" — where AI agents handle the entire purchase flow on behalf of users. This positions Shopify against Meta's Hatch and Amazon's 1P strategy in the battle for in-content commerce.

## Key Relationships
- **Meta / Hatch**: Direct competitor in agentic commerce; both racing to own the "buy from content" flow where users see an item in a Reel or video and purchase it without leaving the content
- **Amazon**: Traditional e-commerce competitor now facing new challenge from in-content shopping; Amazon's 1P (first-party) strategy is being tested by platforms integrating purchase directly into content
- **Stripe**: Core payment infrastructure partner; Shopify's agentic commerce requires similar payment rails for agent-to-agent transactions
