# Grok Gov

## What It Is
Grok Gov is a specialized government version of the Grok AI model, deployed by the U.S. Pentagon for high-stakes military coordination and tactical execution.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Deployer | U.S. Pentagon |
| Use Case | Kinetic Warfare / Target Selection |
| Key Event | Coordinated 2,000 missile strikes in Iran (June 2026) |
| Impact | Automated target selection and timing in OODA loop |

## Significance
Grok Gov marks the first documented large-scale use of AI in the "battlefield execution" phase of kinetic warfare. By automating the OODA (Observe-Orient-Decide-Act) loop at a speed and scale that precludes human-in-the-loop oversight, it transforms AI from a strategic advisor into a tactical executioner.
