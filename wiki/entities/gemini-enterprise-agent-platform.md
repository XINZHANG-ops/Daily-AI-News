# Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform

## What It Is
Google's Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform is a product announced at Cloud Next 2026 for managing thousands of AI agents at scale. It represents Google's answer to the growing enterprise demand for agent orchestration and management infrastructure.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Creator | Google |
| Announcement | Cloud Next 2026 (April 23, 2026) |
| Primary Use | Managing thousands of AI agents at enterprise scale |
| Integration | TPU 8th gen infrastructure |

## Significance
The Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform positions Google as a major player in the emerging agent infrastructure market. With competition from Anthropic's Claude Code, OpenAI's Codex, and Cursor, Google's platform differentiation comes from its integration with Google's TPU infrastructure and the broader Google Cloud ecosystem.

The platform's ability to manage "thousands of agents" signals a shift from single-agent to fleet-level agent management — a necessary capability as enterprises deploy increasingly complex multi-agent systems.
