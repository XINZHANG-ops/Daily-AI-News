# Perplexity Computer

## What It Is
Perplexity Computer is an always-on AI assistant that runs locally on Mac, launched April 17, 2026. It manages files, controls native apps like iMessage and Calendar, and browses web autonomously. Available exclusively to Perplexity Max subscribers at $200/month. Designed to run continuously on a dedicated Mac mini.

## Significance
Perplexity Computer represents a new category of local AI assistant — always-on, device-resident, with control over native applications. The $200/month pricing and dedicated hardware requirement signal a premium positioning for power users who want maximum privacy and capability.
