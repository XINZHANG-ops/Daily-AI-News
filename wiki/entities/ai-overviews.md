# AI Overviews

## What It Is
AI Overviews (and AI Mode) are Google's AI-generated search results that provide direct answers to user queries. On June 3, 2026, Google announced it will begin testing a new toggle in Search Console allowing website owners to exclude their domains from AI-generated search results without affecting regular search placement.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Creator | Google |
| Announcement | June 3, 2026 |
| Opt-Out Mechanism | Search Console toggle |
| Related Features | AI Overviews, AI Mode |

## Significance
This opt-out mechanism is Google's concession to publishers who feared AI Overviews would siphon traffic. Ironically, it may accelerate the very migration it seeks to prevent — publishers already frustrated by zero-click searches may now have less incentive to block AI summaries if regular results remain unaffected.

The move also signals Google's confidence that AI Overviews are good enough that publishers won't want to opt out — or that the damage to the open web is acceptable collateral. This reveals the fundamental tension between AI search and the open web: as AI-generated answers improve, the fracture line between them will only widen.
