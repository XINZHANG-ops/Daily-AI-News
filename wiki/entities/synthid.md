# SynthID

## What It Is
Google's invisible watermarking technology for AI-generated images. OpenAI adopted SynthID in May 2026 as part of joining the C2PA standard, embedding invisible watermarks in AI-generated content to enable detection.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Creator | Google DeepMind |
| Partner | OpenAI (adopted May 2026) |
| Standard | C2PA (Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity) |
| Purpose | Detect AI-generated content |

## Significance
SynthID adoption by OpenAI signals that content provenance is becoming a regulatory requirement rather than a nice-to-have. The EU AI Act mandates content provenance labeling, and Google's watermarking solution provides a proven technical answer. As image generation reaches photorealistic quality, provenance becomes the next battleground — expect Meta and others to follow suit.
