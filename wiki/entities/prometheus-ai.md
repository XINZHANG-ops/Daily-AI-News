# Prometheus

## What It Is
Prometheus is an AI system designed to be an "Artificial General Engineer," capable of performing complex engineering tasks in the physical world.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Release Date | 2026-06-18 |
| Creator | Jeff Bezos |
| Key Metric | $12B funding raised |

## Significance
Prometheus targets the "physical engineer" gap. While other agents focus on digital code, Prometheus aims to bridge the gap between digital reasoning (design/simulation) and robotic execution in physical engineering, representing a push toward full-stack automation of physical infrastructure.
