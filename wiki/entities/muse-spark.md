# Muse Spark

## What It Is
Muse Spark is Meta's first proprietary AI model, released April 8, 2026, breaking with the company's open-source tradition since 2023. It ranks 4th on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index v4.0 with a score of 52.

| Strengths | Figure understanding (86.4% CharXiv), medical reasoning (42.8% HealthBench Hard) |
| Weaknesses | Abstract reasoning (42.5% ARC AGI 2) |

## Significance
Muse Spark's proprietary release marked Meta's break from its open-source tradition, a strategic pivot as Meta simultaneously cut 8,000 jobs (May 20) and ramped capex to $125-145B. Developed under Chief AI Officer Alexandr Wang, formerly of Scale AI, the proprietary shift signals Meta's bet that differentiated model capability creates more enterprise value than open-source ecosystem effects.
