# Mythos 5

## What It Is
Mythos 5 is Anthropic's most powerful frontier model, known for extreme capabilities that often exceed safety thresholds for public release. It is the successor/evolution of the original Mythos project.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Release Date | 2026 |
| Creator | Anthropic |
| Status | Disabled globally by US Government (June 14, 2026) |

## Significance
The US Department of Commerce's order to suspend Mythos 5 globally highlights that "frontier" capability is now viewed as a geopolitical liability and a national security risk if not strictly controlled by the US state.

On June 17, 2026, the G7 summit highlighted the friction this caused, with non-U.S. leaders expressing concern over U.S. control of the model and calling for a "trusted partners" scheme to mitigate the strategic dependency.
