# Eno

## What It Is
Eno is a general-purpose, non-humanoid robot developed by the French startup Genesis AI. It features a wheeled base for efficiency and high-dexterity human-like hands for manipulation, specifically designed for industrial logistics and manufacturing.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Creator | Genesis AI |
| Form Factor | Wheeled base, human-like hands |
| Target Market | Industrial logistics, Manufacturing |
| Philosophy | Pragmatic ROI over human mimicry |

## Significance
Eno represents a pragmatic pivot in the robotics race. While much of the industry focused on humanoids, Eno's design (wheels + hands) optimizes for actual industrial utility and ROI. It signals a realization that the "last mile" of physical agency doesn't require a human shape, just human-level dexterity and efficient mobility.
