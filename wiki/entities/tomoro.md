# Tomoro

## What It Is
Tomoro is an AI deployment company that OpenAI acquired in May 2026 as part of its launch of the Deployment Company. The acquisition brought 150 experienced forward deployed engineers who specialize in embedding AI into enterprise workflows. The deal was valued as part of OpenAI's over $4 billion initial investment in its new deployment business.

## Significance
The acquisition of Tomoro signals OpenAI's strategic shift from being a pure model provider to becoming an enterprise infrastructure company. The 150 experienced engineers represent talent acquisition as much as business acquisition—these are the people who know how to embed AI into existing enterprise workflows.

The $4 billion+ war chest for the Deployment Company suggests aggressive expansion into the $150 billion+ enterprise AI integration market. Unlike a pure API play, this positions OpenAI to own the deployment layer where margins are higher and switching costs create lock-in. It's also a defensive move against Azure and AWS AI services that increasingly commoditize model access.
