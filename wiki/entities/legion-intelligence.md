# Legion Intelligence

## What It Is
Legion Intelligence is a highly specialized system of AI agents developed for integration into U.S. national security workflows. Unlike general-purpose frontier models, it is engineered for precision, reliability, and secure operation within state intelligence and defense frameworks.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Purpose | National Security Workflows |
| Deployer | U.S. Government |
| Status | Deployed |

## Significance
Legion Intelligence represents the institutionalization of "agentic AI" for state power. Its deployment coincides with the restriction of general-purpose frontier models (like Fable 5), suggesting a strategic shift where the state secures a monopoly on the most capable agentic tools while denying them to the general public to prevent adversarial misuse.
