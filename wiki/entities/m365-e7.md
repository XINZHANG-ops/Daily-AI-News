# M365 E7

## What It Is
Microsoft's new enterprise licensing tier launched GA on May 1, 2026 at $99/user/month. Bundles E5 + Copilot + Agent 365 + Entra Suite into a single subscription, making it the first new enterprise Microsoft license tier in a decade. The bundling architecture has been described as a "lock-in masterclass" — enterprises that want AI governance get Microsoft's full stack, and the identity management (Entra) makes switching costs structural.

## Significance
M365 E7 represents the pricing architecture for enterprise AI — governance, identity, and agent access bundled into a single subscription. The key insight is that Entra Suite (identity management) is the strategic component: once AI agent permissions and governance rules are encoded in Microsoft's identity framework, switching away means rebuilding the entire permission structure. This is Microsoft's answer to the question "how do we monetize the agentic AI transition without being undercut by open-source alternatives?"
