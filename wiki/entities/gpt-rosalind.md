# GPT-Rosalind

## What It Is
GPT-Rosalind is OpenAI's reasoning model built specifically for life sciences — biology, drug discovery, and translational medicine. Launched April 16, 2026, with early access partners including Amgen, Moderna, Allen Institute, and Thermo Fisher Scientific. The release came alongside a Life Sciences research plugin for Codex and a strategic partnership with Novo Nordisk.

## Significance
GPT-Rosalind extends OpenAI's pattern of domain-specific models (Cyber for security, Life Sciences for medicine) over general-purpose ones. This specialization strategy allows targeted capabilities for professional domains while maintaining safety boundaries.
