# Alibaba M890

## What It Is
The M890 is Alibaba's latest AI chip designed to challenge Nvidia's dominance in the AI accelerator market, specifically targeting data center inference workloads.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Creator | Alibaba |
| Focus | Data center inference |
| Target Market | AI accelerator market |
| Significance | First credible Chinese challenge to Nvidia in inference |

## Significance
The M890 represents Alibaba's push to reduce dependence on Nvidia chips and establish domestic AI hardware capability. The chip specifically targets inference workloads — the largest and most scalable segment of AI compute — where custom silicon can often outperform general-purpose GPUs.
