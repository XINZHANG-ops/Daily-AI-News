# Stainless SDK

## What It Is
The most popular TypeScript SDK for building Claude-powered applications. Acquired by Anthropic for $300M on May 23, 2026, the SDK is used by thousands of developers building Claude integrations across the AI ecosystem.

## Significance
The Stainless acquisition gives Anthropic the most popular TypeScript SDK in the AI ecosystem. At $300M, it's 6% of Anthropic's $5B valuation — a strategic premium to control the developer tooling layer. This mirrors OpenAI's acquisition pattern (e.g., Rockset, Evalian) but with a crucial difference: Stainless is already deeply integrated with Claude, making it more of a "defensive" acquisition to prevent competitor adoption.

The move signals Anthropic's shift from model-only to platform strategy. By owning the SDK, Anthropic controls how developers interact with Claude at the tooling level — this prevents competitors from building better developer experiences on top of Anthropic's models. The $300M price tag reflects not just the SDK's current usage but the strategic value of controlling the developer relationship. For comparison, OpenAI's acquisition of Rockset (database) and Evalian (evaluation platform) served similar platform-control purposes.
