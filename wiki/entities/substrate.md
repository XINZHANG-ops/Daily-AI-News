# Substrate

## What It Is
Substrate is a Kubernetes-based system for managing agent-like workloads. It multiplexes actors onto workers to achieve high scale with lower latency, enabling efficient orchestration of large-scale AI agent deployments.

 Substrate addresses the infrastructure challenge of deploying AI agents at scale. As organizations deploy more AI agents, the need for efficient workload orchestration becomes critical.
