# Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS

## What It Is
Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS is Google's text-to-speech release from April 2026 (approximately April 15-20). It offers granular control over speaking style, pace, pitch, and emphasis via natural language prompting. Designed for podcast production, audiobook generation, and accessibility tools.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Release Date | 2026-04-15 to 2026-04-20 |
| Creator | Google |
| Focus | Natural language voice control |
| Use Cases | Podcast production, audiobooks, accessibility |

## Significance
The granular natural language control over voice synthesis represents a significant advancement in TTS usability. Developers can now specify voice characteristics in plain language rather than through technical parameters.
