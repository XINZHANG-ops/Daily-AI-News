# Andrej Karpathy

## Who He Is
OpenAI cofounder and former Tesla AI director. Joined Anthropic in May 2026 as Pretraining Advisor to accelerate research using Claude. Known for founding OpenAI's original research team and building Tesla's Autopilot AI division from scratch.

## Significance
Karpathy's move from OpenAI to Anthropic is the highest-profile departure from OpenAI to date. His hiring gives Anthropic access to deep expertise in both large-scale pretraining (from OpenAI) and real-world AI deployment (from Tesla). The hire represents Anthropic's two-pronged strategy: accelerate frontier capabilities (Karpathy) while addressing safety concerns (previously hired Jan Leike from OpenAI's safety team).
