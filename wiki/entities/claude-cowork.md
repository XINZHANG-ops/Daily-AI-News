# Claude Cowork

## What It Is
Claude Cowork is Anthropic's enterprise agentic AI product for non-coding professional workflows, deployed alongside Claude Code as part of the company's enterprise suite. PwC's "Office of the CFO" — a new business group built entirely on Claude technology — will deploy Claude Cowork to hundreds of thousands of professionals globally, alongside Claude Code for technical staff.

## Significance
Claude Cowork represents Anthropic's expansion beyond coding agents into general enterprise productivity — the same trajectory OpenSwarm and other vertical agents are pursuing. PwC building an entire "Office of the CFO" business unit "entirely on Claude" is a structural bet, not a pilot program: it signals that AI is transitioning from "tool you use" to "infrastructure you build on." The 70% delivery time reduction cited by PwC (insurance underwriting: 10 weeks to 10 days; cybersecurity response: hours to minutes) demonstrates that agentic AI is moving from experimentation to core business infrastructure. This is the kind of operational deployment that validates Anthropic's enterprise lead over OpenAI.
