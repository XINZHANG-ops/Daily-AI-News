# Dataroom

## What It Is
A research harness that leverages local models (such as Qwen3.6) to autonomously build comprehensive, cited knowledge archives (called "datarooms") in .zip format. It transforms the process of research from searching for fragments to constructing grounded, archival knowledge bases.

## Significance
Dataroom exemplifies the "local-first" research paradigm. By using high-performance local models to synthesize cited archives, it reduces dependence on volatile cloud-based frontier models and provides a permanent, verifiable record of knowledge that is immune to external "digital kill switches" or API restrictions.
