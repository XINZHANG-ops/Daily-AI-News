# Grok 4.3

## What It Is
Grok 4.3 is xAI's successor to Grok 3, launched May 6, 2026 with an 83% price cut. The model adds video input (up to 5 minutes), document output (PDF/XLSX/PPTX), and always-on reasoning. Also announced: voice cloning from 120-second samples and connectors for iOS/Android. Grok 3 was deprecated effective May 15, 2026.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |

| Output Price | $2.50/1M tokens |

| Deprecation | Grok 3 deprecated May 15, 2026 |

## Significance
At $1.25/1M input tokens, Grok 4.3 undercuts GPT-4.5 by roughly 60% while offering 1M token context and video understanding. This is not a niche product launch — it's a strategic price war. xAI's pricing follows the pattern of ElevenLabs' aggressive pricing (announced the same week) and signals that the AI API market is entering a race-to-the-bottom phase where only compute-rich providers survive.

The 83% price cut is classic Musk market-entry strategy: undercut incumbents to gain share, then monetize through vertical integration. The document output feature (PDF/XLSX/PPTX) directly targets enterprise workflows, while video input extends Grok into multimodal territory previously dominated by GPT-4.5 and Gemini.
