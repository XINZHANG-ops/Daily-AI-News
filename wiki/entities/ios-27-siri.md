# iOS 27 Siri

## What It Is
The biggest Siri overhaul in years, coming at WWDC 2026 with the tagline "Coming Bright Up". iOS 27 introduces a dedicated Siri app with persistent chat threads, web search capabilities, document summarization, and deeper app control. Most significantly, it introduces an Extensions framework allowing users to install rival AI chatbots and choose their preferred AI model as the default assistant.

## Key Features
- Dedicated Siri app with persistent chat threads
- Web search integration
- Document summarization
- Deeper app control and automation
- **Extensions framework** — First time users can install rival AI chatbots
- Model selection — Choose preferred AI (Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude) as default

## Significance
This represents Apple's most significant AI pivot yet. The $250M Siri lawsuit settlement (over delayed features promised with iPhone 16) likely accelerated this opening-up strategy. For the first time, Apple is breaking its traditional walled garden to allow third-party AI alternatives to become default assistants. This could fundamentally change how users interact with AI on iOS devices — from Apple's single assistant to a competitive marketplace.

With John Ternus taking over as CEO in September 2026, this Siri redesign may be Tim Cook's final legacy play in AI — and it's notably more open than anything Apple has done before.
