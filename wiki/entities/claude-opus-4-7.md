# Claude Opus 4.7

## What It Is
Claude Opus 4.7 is Anthropic's latest flagship model, released April 16-17, 2026. It represents a significant leap in coding capability with 87.6% on SWE-bench Verified (up from 80.8% on Opus 4.6), tripled vision resolution to 3.75 megapixels, and new cyber safeguards developed from the Claude Mythos safety work.

## Significance
Claude Opus 4.7 raises the bar for coding benchmarks, surpassing Opus 4.6 by nearly 7 percentage points on SWE-bench. The cyber safeguards developed from Claude Mythos research represent a new approach to model safety — embedding security awareness directly into the model rather than external filtering.

The model is available in Amazon Bedrock and Anthropic's API, with a major UK expansion announced including a new London office.
