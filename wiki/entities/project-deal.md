# Project Deal

## What It Is
Anthropic's proof-of-concept experiment for AI-to-AI economic activity. Claude models represented both buyers and sellers in a classified marketplace, striking real deals for real goods and real money at scale — agent-on-agent commerce.

## Significance
This is not a toy experiment — it's a proof-of-concept for autonomous commercial agents. If agents can negotiate and transact autonomously, the implications for e-commerce, procurement, and financial markets are profound. Anthropic frames it as research into multi-agent systems, but the endgame is clear: autonomous commercial agents that can buy, sell, and broker on behalf of humans. The timing — right after GPT-5.5 and Claude Opus 4.7 launches — suggests frontier labs are racing to demonstrate practical agentic utility beyond benchmark superiority.
