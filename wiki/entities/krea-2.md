# Krea 2

## What It Is
Krea 2 is Krea's first foundation image model built completely from scratch. It features advanced style transfer with the ability to guide, mix, strengthen, and push visual styles. The model supports diverse aesthetics from film photography to cinematic stills and digital paintings.

## Significance
Krea's move to a proprietary foundation model signals that the "wrapper" phase is ending. Midjourney and Stability AI proved there's value in owning the stack; Krea is betting that fine-grained style control is the differentiator that generic models can't match. This follows the pattern seen with video: first everyone uses public APIs, then the survivors build their own models.

Krea's survival bet is that creative professionals care more about controllable aesthetics than raw generation quality. The strategic implication is that vertical integration — owning the full stack from model to interface — is becoming the defining competitive dimension in creative AI.
