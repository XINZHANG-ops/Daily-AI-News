# AlphaProof Nexus

## What It Is
AlphaProof Nexus is Google DeepMind's mathematical reasoning model specifically designed to solve open problems in mathematics. It combines reinforcement learning with formal proof systems to attack unsolved mathematical conjectures.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Creator | Google DeepMind |
| Release Date | May 22, 2026 |
| Primary Use | Mathematical theorem proving and open problem solving |
| Performance | Solved 9 of 353 open Erdős problems; proved 44 of 492 OEIS sequence conjectures |
| Cost | $100-200 per problem (orders of magnitude cheaper than traditional mathematicians) |

## Significance
AlphaProof Nexus represents a watershed moment in AI-driven mathematical discovery. On the same day as OpenAI's Erdős breakthrough, AlphaProof Nexus demonstrated that AI reasoning systems can autonomously solve open mathematical problems at a fraction of human cost. The $100-200 price tag per problem compared to the traditional mathematician cost of thousands of dollars per problem signals a fundamental shift in how mathematical research may be conducted. Combined with OpenAI's achievement, this establishes AI as a genuine tool for original mathematical research rather than just computation.
