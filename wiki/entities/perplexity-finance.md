# Perplexity Finance

## What It Is
Perplexity Finance is an AI platform targeting financial analysts and investment firms, launched May 6, 2026. It integrates licensed data from Morningstar, PitchBook, Daloopa, and Carbon Arc, featuring 40+ live finance tools, 35 prebuilt analyst workflows (equity comparison, screening, report generation), and traceable outputs with citations to SEC filings, transcripts, and licensed databases. It introduces a "bring your own license" model allowing firms to use existing data subscriptions.

## Significance
Perplexity Finance is the most credible challenge to Bloomberg Terminal's 40-year dominance since Refinitiv. The "bring your own license" model is the key innovation — most buy-side firms already pay $25,000+/year per seat for Morningstar, PitchBook, and FactSet licenses. Perplexity isn't asking them to drop those subscriptions; it's offering to orchestrate them with natural language queries and AI-generated analysis. The 35 prebuilt workflows target the exact tasks that junior analysts spend 60+ hours/week on. The citation-first approach makes every output auditable — a direct attack on Bloomberg's moat of social proof. The timing is perfect: OpenAI's GPT-5.5 Instant launched the same day with similar citation features, but Perplexity has the data partnerships and finance-specific workflows.
