# TradingAgents

## What It Is
TradingAgents is an open-source multi-agent LLM framework for autonomous financial trading, reaching 54.6K stars on GitHub. The project deploys specialized agents for research, technical analysis, risk management, sentiment analysis, and portfolio management — orchestrated to make autonomous trading decisions without human intervention at each step.

## Significance
TradingAgents represents the frontier of autonomous economic agency: an open-source project that handles the full lifecycle of financial decision-making — from research to execution — with multiple specialized agents debating and validating each trade. Its 54.6K stars prove that developers are not just experimenting with agentic finance; they are building production-grade infrastructure for it. The project validates the broader thesis that vertical agents outperform general-purpose assistants in specific domains: a general LLM cannot safely trade, but a fleet of specialized agents (researcher, risk manager, trader) can.
