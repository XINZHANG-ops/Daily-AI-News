# AFM 3 Cloud Pro

## What It Is
AFM 3 Cloud Pro is the cloud-tier flagship of Apple's AFM 3 family, announced at WWDC 2026 on June 8. It runs on NVIDIA-backed cloud infrastructure, marking Apple's first explicit cloud-NVIDIA dependency since the PowerPC-to-Intel and Intel-to-Apple Silicon transitions. AFM 3 Cloud Pro is targeted at the most demanding Apple Intelligence workloads that exceed on-device sparse MoE capacity.

## Significance
AFM 3 Cloud Pro on NVIDIA is the third Apple platform pivot in three decades. After PowerPC→Intel and Intel→Apple Silicon, Apple is now relying on NVIDIA in the cloud for its most capable model — a structural reversal of the vertical-integration philosophy that defined Apple Silicon. The implication: even Apple, with its $3T+ market cap, cannot economically serve frontier-class AI workloads entirely on its own silicon at scale.

The NVIDIA dependency is also notable geopolitically. Apple has been one of the most insulated US tech companies from the NVIDIA supply chain drama; AFM 3 Cloud Pro directly couples Apple's AI roadmap to NVIDIA's allocation and export-control posture. If Anthropic commits $200B to Google Cloud (May 5) and now Apple commits to NVIDIA, the cloud-AI capex story is concentrated in 2-3 vendors.
