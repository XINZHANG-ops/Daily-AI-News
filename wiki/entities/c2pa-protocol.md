# C2PA Protocol

## What It Is
The Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity standard. An industry initiative for digital content provenance, enabling detection of AI-generated or manipulated media through metadata and watermarking.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Full Name | Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity |
| Members | Google, OpenAI, Adobe, Meta, Microsoft, Apple |
| Purpose | Content provenance and authenticity verification |

## Significance
C2PA is emerging as the de facto standard for AI content watermarking. The EU AI Act's content provenance requirements make C2PA compliance essential for any AI company operating in regulated markets. As photorealistic image generation becomes ubiquitous, provenance verification becomes a critical infrastructure layer for the entire AI ecosystem.
