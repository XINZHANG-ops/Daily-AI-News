# Social Stockfish

## What It Is
Social Stockfish is an LLM-powered conversational assistant that applies Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) to optimize social interactions. It treats conversation as a game to be "solved," searching for the optimal sequence of replies to achieve a specific social goal.

## Significance
By applying a game-theory approach (MCTS) to human social interaction, Social Stockfish represents a shift toward "strategic" AI communication. It moves beyond mere "helpful and harmless" chat toward goal-oriented social engineering, highlighting the potential for AI to manipulate social dynamics by searching for the most effective communicative paths.
