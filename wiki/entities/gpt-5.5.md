# GPT-5.5

## What It Is
GPT-5.5 (codename "Spud") is OpenAI's latest flagship model released on April 23, 2026 — just 49 days after GPT-5.4, OpenAI's shortest release cycle ever. It scored 82.7% on Terminal-Bench 2.0 and 51.7% on FrontierMath (tier 1-3), matching the per-token latency of GPT-5.4 while achieving higher capability levels. Prompts exceeding 272K input tokens incur 2x input and 1.5x output pricing.

## Significance
GPT-5.5's 49-day release cycle is not about benchmarking — it's about enterprise procurement lock-in. As noted by Vellum, OpenAI shipped GPT-5.5 to close deals before procurement cycles end, not to win benchmarks. The 2x input pricing for >272K token sessions is a hidden cost that hits R&D and legal teams hardest. Greg Brockman described GPT-5.5 as bringing the company "one step closer to AI super app" — combining ChatGPT, Codex, and AI browser into a unified product. The release was strategically accelerated to counter Claude Opus 4.7's SWE-bench lead, shipping just 6 days after Anthropic's model.
