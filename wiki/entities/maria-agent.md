# Maria Agent

## What It Is
Maria is an agentic AI integrated with a high-throughput laboratory, specifically designed for medicinal chemistry. It manages the loop from hypothesis generation to physical lab execution.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Release Date | 2026-06-18 |
| Creator | Molecule.one / OpenAI |
| Key Metric | 10,000+ experiments automated |

## Significance
Maria represents the transition from AI as a research assistant to AI as a lead chemist. By closing the loop with physical hardware, it can iteratively optimize chemical reactions (e.g., the Chan-Lam coupling reaction) at a scale and speed impossible for humans.
