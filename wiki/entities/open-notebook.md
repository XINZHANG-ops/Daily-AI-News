# Open Notebook

## What It Is
Open Notebook is an open-source, self-hosted alternative to Google Notebook LM. It supports over 18 AI providers and includes a multi-speaker podcast generation feature, allowing users to create structured knowledge bases with an open ecosystem of LLMs.

## Significance
The emergence of Open Notebook reflects a growing demand for "Sovereign AI" tools—applications that can be self-hosted to avoid dependency on proprietary clouds (like Google's) and to maintain full control over data and model choice in a fragmented geopolitical landscape.
