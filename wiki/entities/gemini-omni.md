# Gemini Omni

## What It Is
Google's world model for physical simulation. Released at Google I/O 2026, Gemini Omni generates video outputs for real-world interaction — competing with World Labs' physical simulation efforts. The model processes text, audio, images, and video together with conversational video editing capabilities.

## Significance
Gemini Omni's "world model" capability — generating video outputs that model physical reality — echoes DeepMind's work on GameNGen but extends to general physical simulation. This positions Google in competition with World Labs and potentially Apple (via robotics). The world model capability is essential for any AI system that needs to understand and predict physical outcomes — from robotics to self-driving to scientific simulation.

The May 20, 2026 update adds conversational video editing — enabling users to edit video through natural language conversation. This suggests Google is targeting creative workflows, not just video generation. The ability to have a dialogue about video editing while the model understands both the visual content and the semantic intent positions Gemini Omni as both a creative tool and a step toward more natural human-AI interaction in visual domains. Combined with the "world model" framing, this signals Google is building toward AI that doesn't just generate content but understands and simulates physical reality.
