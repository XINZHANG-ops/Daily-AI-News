# DeepSeek-V4

## What It Is
DeepSeek-V4 is DeepSeek's latest flagship model family released on April 24, 2026. Available in two variants: V4-Pro (1.6T total parameters with 49B active params per inference) and V4-Flash (284B total with 13B active params). The models feature enhanced agentic coding, world-class reasoning, and 1M token context as default. Open weights are available on Hugging Face.

## Significance
DeepSeek-V4 represents DeepSeek's continued challenge to US AI labs at a fraction of the investment. The V4-Flash variant (13B active params) bringing 1M context as default demonstrates aggressive efficiency optimization. Open weights availability continues DeepSeek's strategy of making frontier-class models accessible to the broader research community. The timing — alongside news of Tencent and Alibaba investing — signals growing Chinese tech industry validation of DeepSeek's technical approach.

By April 28, DeepSeek V4-Pro-Max has emerged as the cost-performance leader at $1.74/M tokens — 2.6x cheaper than Claude Opus 4.7 and 17x cheaper than GPT-5.5's output pricing. This pricing pressure is particularly significant as OpenAI reportedly missed Q1 revenue targets after losing enterprise market share to Anthropic's Claude series.
