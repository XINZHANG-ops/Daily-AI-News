# Magma

## What It Is
Microsoft's foundation model for multimodal AI agents, presented at CVPR 2025. Designed to handle both digital UI navigation (virtual tasks) and physical robotics (real-world tasks) — a unified foundation for agentic AI across digital and physical domains.

## Significance
Magma represents Microsoft's answer to the question of how to unify digital and physical AI agents. Unlike specialized models that handle either virtual tasks or robotics, Magma is designed as a single foundation that can drive both — potentially creating a "Android for robotics" software licensing play similar to Android's position in mobile.
