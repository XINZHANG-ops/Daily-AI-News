# MOVA

## What It Is
MOVA is an open-source foundation model from OpenMOSS that generates synchronized video and audio in a single inference pass, breaking the "silent era" of open-source video generation. It features precise multilingual lip-sync, environment-aware sound effects, ComfyUI integration, and SGLang support.

## Significance
MOVA represents a step toward closing the gap between open-source and proprietary video generation models. Synchronized audio-visual generation in a single pass eliminates the post-processing pipeline that previously required separate video and audio models, making it a meaningful infrastructure advance for the open-source AI video ecosystem.
