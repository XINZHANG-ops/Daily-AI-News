# JoyAI-Echo

## What It Is
`jd-opensource/JoyAI-Echo` is a tool for long-form audio-visual generation. It utilizes a cross-modal memory bank to ensure that characters and voices remain consistent across multi-shot stories, solving one of the primary hurdles in AI cinematography: temporal consistency.

## Significance
JoyAI-Echo pushes the boundary of "generative cinema" by moving from single-shot generation to structured, consistent storytelling. The use of a dedicated memory bank for modalities (visual and audio) is a key step toward coherent AI-generated long-form content.
