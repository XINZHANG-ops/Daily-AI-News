# ARI (Assured Robot Intelligence)

## What It Is
Meta's humanoid robotics acquisition, announced May 1, 2026. The team, led by Xiaolong Wang and Lerrel Pinto, was absorbed into Meta's Superintelligence Labs under Yann LeCun. ARI extends the AI job displacement thesis from digital knowledge work to physical labor — the same week Meta announced 8,000 layoffs.

## Significance
ARI marks the convergence of AI-driven job displacement and physical automation. Meta's simultaneous announcement of 8,000 layoffs and a robotics acquisition makes the pattern explicit: digital knowledge workers are being replaced by LLM-based agents while the infrastructure for replacing physical workers is being acquired. The absorption into LeCun's Superintelligence Labs signals Meta views robotics as an AI problem, not a mechanical engineering problem — the same model architectures that replace coders will eventually replace warehouse workers.
