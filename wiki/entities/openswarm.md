# OpenSwarm

## What It Is
OpenSwarm is a fully open-source multi-agent system built on Agency Swarm. It creates polished deliverables — slide decks, reports, visualizations, documents, and videos — from a single prompt using 8 specialized agents with 10,000+ integrations via Composio.

## Significance
OpenSwarm occupies a distinct niche in the agent ecosystem: while Claude Code, Codex, and Cursor dominate coding workflows, OpenSwarm targets the broader knowledge-work layer that most professionals actually spend their time on. Its multi-agent architecture — with dedicated roles for research, design, writing, and review — mirrors how human teams collaborate on deliverables. The "non-coding" positioning is strategically important: it signals that agentic AI is maturing beyond software development into general office productivity, a market orders of magnitude larger than developer tools. The 10,000+ Composio integrations make it a serious enterprise contender rather than a toy project.
