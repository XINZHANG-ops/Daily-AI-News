# OpenSwarm

## What It Is
OpenSwarm is an open-source multi-agent system for non-coding work — presentations, documents, research reports, and visual assets. It orchestrates 8 specialized agents including an Orchestrator, Deep Research, Designer, Writer, and Reviewer, enabling end-to-end automation of knowledge-work workflows that traditional coding agents do not address.

## Significance
OpenSwarm occupies a distinct niche in the agent ecosystem: while Claude Code, Codex, and Cursor dominate coding workflows, OpenSwarm targets the broader knowledge-work layer that most professionals actually spend their time on. Its multi-agent architecture — with dedicated roles for research, design, writing, and review — mirrors how human teams collaborate on deliverables. The "non-coding" positioning is strategically important: it signals that agentic AI is maturing beyond software development into general office productivity, a market orders of magnitude larger than developer tools.
