# Gemini Spark

## What It Is
Google's always-on personal AI agent for Ultra subscribers. Launched May 2026 at Google I/O as an always-on personal agent that proactively assists users throughout their day rather than waiting for explicit prompts. Positioned as a proactive personal agent rather than reactive assistant — distinguishing it from Claude's always-on memory and Anthropic's computer use.

## Significance
Gemini Spark represents Google's answer to Claude's always-on memory and Anthropic's computer use — but positioned differently. Where Claude waits for user interaction, Spark proactively monitors and assists. The Antigravity platform released alongside is the real story: it's a complete agent runtime with privacy controls, suggesting Google is targeting enterprise adoption more than consumers. This positions them differently from OpenAI's consumer-focused ChatGPT strategy and Anthropic's professional developer focus.

The always-on nature creates a fundamental shift: instead of "ask and receive," the AI becomes a persistent presence that anticipates needs. For Google, this leverages their data advantages (Calendar, Gmail, Maps, Search history) in ways competitors can't match.
