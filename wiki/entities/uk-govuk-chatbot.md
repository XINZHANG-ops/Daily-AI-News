# UK GOV.UK Chatbot

## What It Is
The UK government's AI chatbot integrated into the GOV.UK App (563K users). Built on Anthropic's Claude LLM, it provides instant answers from 80,000 pages of official guidance with 90% accuracy. Top queries: tax, driving, transport, benefits.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Launch Date | 2026-05-15 |
| Creator | UK Government (built on Anthropic Claude) |
| Platform | GOV.UK App |
| Users | 563,000 |
| Accuracy | 90% |
| Development Time | 2.5 years |

## Significance
After 2.5 years of development, the UK government's Claude-powered chatbot represents one of the most significant government AI deployments to date. The choice of Claude over open-source alternatives signals confidence in Anthropic's safety guardrails for high-stakes citizen interactions — this is not a technical decision but a trust decision.

The 90% accuracy figure, while impressive, means approximately 10,000 potential failures daily at the scale some call centers handle. The 2.5-year timeline suggests either extreme caution or internal friction in government technology procurement. Built on Claude rather than open-source models, this creates a dependency on Anthropic's infrastructure for critical government services.

The deployment gives the UK a narrative win on "AI-powered governance" compared to the US, where federal AI adoption remains fragmented. However, the implementation risks — accuracy at scale, vendor dependency, and the lack of EU regulatory access to Claude Mythos — create structural vulnerabilities.
