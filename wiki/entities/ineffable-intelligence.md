# Ineffable Intelligence

## What It Is
AI startup founded by ex-DeepMind researcher David Silver (co-author of the AlphaGo paper) that raised the largest seed round in AI history at $1.1 billion in April 2026. The company has not disclosed what it is building, but the investor syndicate includes Sequoia, Lightspeed, Nvidia, Google, and DST Global.

## Significance
David Silver brings the DeepMind RL lineage (AlphaGo, AlphaFold) to a new venture. The AlphaFold breakthrough demonstrated that DeepMind could produce transformative AI research; investors are betting Silver can do it again in a new context. The $1.1B seed without a disclosed product signals extreme confidence in near-AGI timelines. Compare this to OpenAI's $7B raised in 2025 — the bar for what's considered a credible AGI-adjacent startup has shifted dramatically upward.

The investor syndicate is notable: Sequoia + Lightspeed (top-tier VC), Nvidia (AI compute), Google (AI infrastructure), DST Global + Index (international capital). All five in the same round suggests a consensus bet on a specific outcome.
