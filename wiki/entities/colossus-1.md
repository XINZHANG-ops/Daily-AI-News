# Colossus 1

## What It Is
SpaceX's Colossus 1 is a 300+ megawatt data center in Memphis, Tennessee, housing 220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs. It is the physical infrastructure backing Anthropic's compute partnership with SpaceX, enabling the immediate doubling of Claude Code rate limits across all paid tiers and the removal of peak-hour throttling.

## Significance
Colossus 1 represents the physical reality of the compute shortage that is restructuring AI competitive dynamics. When Anthropic needed immediate capacity to solve service reliability issues, SpaceX had unused power at Colossus 1 — making the partnership possible. The 220,000+ GPU count in a single facility is unprecedented scale, and it gives Anthropic dedicated capacity that OpenAI lacks.

The facility also signals Musk's strategic pivot from "build a model company" (xAI, now dissolved) to "own the infrastructure that model companies need." By providing 300+ MW to Anthropic while dissolving his own AI lab, Musk is admitting that infrastructure, not models, is the strategic asset.
