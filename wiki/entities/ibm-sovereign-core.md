# IBM Sovereign Core

## What It Is
IBM's runtime policy embedding system that enforces data sovereignty and regulatory compliance at the infrastructure level rather than as an add-on. Enterprises can deploy the same AI agent across EU, US, and APAC regions with different compliance profiles automatically enforced at runtime — no code changes required.

| Companion Products | watsonx Orchestrate, Concert |

## Significance
Sovereign Core is the most strategically significant piece of IBM's AI governance stack. It directly addresses the EU AI Act's €800M fine on Meta (April 29) and the Five Eyes agent-security guidance (May 1) by embedding regulatory policy into infrastructure rather than bolting it on top. This is IBM betting that enterprises will pay a premium for governance-first AI — competing with Microsoft's E7 bundle ($99/user/month) on compliance rather than price. The risk is that open-source governance frameworks may commoditize the layer IBM wants to monetize before IBM ships at scale.
