# Midjourney Medical

## What It Is
Midjourney Medical is a hardware division of Midjourney that produces AI-powered medical imaging devices, starting with a full-body ultrasonic scanner.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Release Date | 2026-06-18 |
| Creator | Midjourney |
| Key Metric | 3D body maps in <60 seconds |

## Significance
This marks a strategic pivot for Midjourney from generating synthetic 2D art to reconstructing real-world 3D biological structures. It bets that generative latent space mastery can be applied to extreme-efficiency medical imaging, potentially disrupting the MRI market.
