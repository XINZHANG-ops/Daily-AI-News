# ChatGPT

## What It Is
OpenAI's primary consumer AI interface. In June 2026, it expanded its capabilities by integrating the Visa payment network, allowing AI agents within the platform to independently shop and pay for users.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Creator | OpenAI |
| Integration | Visa Payment Network |
| Capability | Autonomous Purchasing |

## Significance
The integration of financial rails marks the transition of ChatGPT from a "Recommendation Engine" or "Planner" to an "Economic Actor". By granting agents the ability to execute financial transactions, OpenAI has effectively solved the "last mile" of agentic AI: the ability to act on the world with real economic consequence.
