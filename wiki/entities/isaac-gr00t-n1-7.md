# Isaac GR00T N1.7

## What It Is
NVIDIA's foundation model for generalist robots. Vision-language-action (VLA) model designed to enable humanoid robots to understand and execute a wide range of physical tasks through visual and language inputs.

## Significance
Isaac GR00T N1.7 represents NVIDIA's push into physical AI — bridging the gap between digital AI capabilities and real-world robotic manipulation. Together with Microsoft Magma and VITA-1.5, these robotics foundation models signal that the next frontier is physical world interaction where multimodal understanding meets real-world action.
