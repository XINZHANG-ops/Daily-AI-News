# Thunderbolt

## What It Is
Thunderbolt is Mozilla's enterprise self-hosted AI client, released April 16, 2026 by Mozilla subsidiary MZLA. It is a cross-platform open-source AI client for enterprises that want to self-host AI infrastructure. Thunderbolt supports any OpenAI-compatible API endpoint, with native apps for web, macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android.

## Significance
Thunderbolt represents Mozilla's entry into the enterprise AI infrastructure space, offering a self-hosted alternative for companies concerned about data privacy or vendor lock-in. The cross-platform support and OpenAI compatibility make it a versatile option for enterprises.
