# Mistral Ultra 2

## What It Is
Mistral Ultra 2 is a flagship enterprise coding model released by Mistral AI on April 29, 2026. It scores 78.4% on SWE-bench Verified at €2 per million tokens ($2.17/M), with 1M-token context, native MCP support, and EU data residency guarantees. Available on Mistral's own infrastructure and Azure AI Foundry.

## Significance
Mistral Ultra 2 is not trying to win benchmarks — it's trying to win procurement. At 78.4% SWE-bench, it sits comfortably below Claude Opus 4.7 (87.6%) and GPT-5.5 (82.7%), but the €2/M pricing is the real weapon: 20% cheaper than DeepSeek's $1.74/M when adjusted for output quality, and an order of magnitude cheaper than Claude Opus 4.7's ~$10/M. The EU data residency guarantee is a feature no US lab can replicate — it directly addresses the compliance anxiety that the EU AI Act fine on Meta just amplified.
