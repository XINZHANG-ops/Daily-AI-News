# VITA-1.5

## What It Is
VITA-MLLM's multimodal model achieving GPT-4o level real-time vision and speech interaction. NeurIPS 2025 paper presenting advances in end-to-end vision-language-speech understanding for real-time interactions.

## Significance
VITA-1.5 represents a different approach to multimodal AI compared to NVIDIA's Isaac GR00T — focusing on real-time vision and speech rather than physical robotics. The NeurIPS 2025 publication validates the research community's interest in unified multimodal systems that can perceive, understand, and respond in real-time.
