# Gemini 3

## What It Is
Google's frontier LLM released May 20, 2026, delivering 2.8x inference speed and 47% lower price compared to its predecessor. At $0.53/M input tokens, it undercuts both OpenAI and Anthropic's offerings.

| Attribute | Value |

| Price Cut | 47% vs Gemini 2.5 |

## Significance
Gemini 3's aggressive pricing signals the start of a frontier model price war. By undercutting OpenAI (GPT-5) and Anthropic (Claude 4 Opus at $15/M), Google occupies the price-performance sweet spot that GPT-5 was meant to claim. The delay of GPT-5 (missed early May release) left OpenAI vulnerable, and Gemini 3 exploits that gap.
