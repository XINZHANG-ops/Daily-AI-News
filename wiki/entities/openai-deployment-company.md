# OpenAI Deployment Company

## What It Is
The OpenAI Deployment Company is a new business unit launched by OpenAI in May 2026 to focus on enterprise AI deployment and integration. Backed by over $4 billion in initial investment, it represents OpenAI's strategic shift from being a pure model provider to owning the deployment layer where higher margins and stronger lock-in exist.

## Significance
The Deployment Company marks OpenAI's bid to control the full AI stack — from model training to enterprise integration. By owning deployment infrastructure, OpenAI captures more value per customer and creates switching costs that pure API access cannot match.

The $4B+ war chest signals aggressive expansion into the $150B+ enterprise AI integration market. This positions OpenAI directly against Microsoft Azure and AWS AI services, which increasingly commoditize model access.

## Key Acquisition: Tomoro
In May 2026, OpenAI acquired Tomoro, bringing 150 experienced forward deployed engineers who specialize in embedding AI into enterprise workflows. This represents both talent acquisition and business capability expansion.
