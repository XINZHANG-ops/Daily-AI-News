# FuriosaAI

## What It Is
FuriosaAI is a South Korean AI chip startup developing accelerators to challenge Nvidia's dominance in the AI hardware market. The company's new chip directly targets the inference market, representing a credible challenge to Nvidia's 80%+ market share.

## Significance
FuriosaAI represents the first credible challenge from South Korea to Nvidia's AI chip dominance. Alongside Alibaba's M890, these chips signal that Nvidia's competitive moat is finally seeing real competition — not just from a single company but from a fragmented market of custom silicon targeting inference specifically.
