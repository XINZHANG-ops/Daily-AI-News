# harmonist

## What It Is
A portable AI agent orchestration framework with mechanical protocol enforcement, developed by GammaLabTechnologies. It features 186 curated specialist agents across 16 categories, zero third-party dependencies, and IDE hook-based workflow enforcement for Cursor, Claude Code, Copilot, and more.

## Significance
harmonist's "zero third-party dependencies" philosophy signals a new trend in agent frameworks: after the MCP vulnerability (150M+ installs) and npm compromises (LiteLLM, axios), teams are seeking to minimize supply chain risk. The mechanical protocol enforcement approach suggests a more structured, auditable agent orchestration model rather than the emergent behavior typical of LLM-based agents.

The 186 specialist agents across 16 categories represents a large-scale attempt to provide domain-specific agents that can be composed into workflows, similar to the scientific-agent-skills pattern but for general-purpose agentic work.
