# Microsoft Copilot

## What It Is
Microsoft's AI assistant integrated across its product ecosystem — from Windows to Office 365 to Azure. Positioned as the enterprise AI standard, Copilot spans consumer (Copilot in Windows, Edge) and enterprise (Copilot for Microsoft 365, Copilot Studio, Agent 365) tiers.

## Significance
Microsoft Copilot is the cornerstone of Microsoft's AI strategy — integrated into the world's most used enterprise productivity suite. The non-exclusive 2032 partnership with OpenAI allows competitors like ChatGPT to embed in Office, forcing Copilot to compete on capability rather than exclusivity. Copilot Cowork (powered by Anthropic/Claude) shows Microsoft hedging its AI bets by partnering with rivals for workflow orchestration.
