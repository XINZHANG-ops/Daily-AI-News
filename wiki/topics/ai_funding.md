# AI Funding

A comprehensive overview of AI funding rounds, valuations, and investment trends.

## Record Funding Environment

### Q1 2026 Summary
- **Total VC funding**: $267B (more than double previous quarterly record)
- **AI share**: ~80% of all global VC funding ($242B)
- Compared to Q1 2025: $59.6B (4x increase)

This represents an unprecedented concentration of capital into AI, driven by the belief that AI infrastructure will be as transformative as electricity or the internet.

## Investment Themes

### 1. Compute as Strategic Asset
AI compute is being treated like energy security - a strategic asset that cannot be outsourced. This drives:
- Vertical integration (SpaceX + xAI)
- Long-term supply agreements (Google + Anthropic TPUs)
- Sovereign AI initiatives

### 2. Agentic AI Infrastructure
Funding flowing to:
- Agent frameworks (Microsoft Agent Framework v1.0, CrewAI)
- Multi-agent orchestration
- Agent security (Oasis Security $120M)

### 3. Open Source Security
Linux Foundation: $12.5M in grants from Anthropic, AWS, GitHub, Google, Microsoft, OpenAI for Alpha-Omega and OpenSSF initiatives.

### 4. International Competition
- US-China AI competition driving investment
- Europe's push for sovereign AI (Nebius Finland data center)
- Japan's Microsoft $10B bet
